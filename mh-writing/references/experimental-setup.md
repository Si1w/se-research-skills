# Experimental Setup

## Purpose
Explain how you propose to demonstrate that your solution addresses the problem.

## Required Content
- Subjects on which you chose to evaluate
- Statistical tests or other measurements and techniques applied
- Research questions you set out to ask, and the motivation for asking them

## Separation of Concerns
- Do not pollute this section with results or details of the proposed solution.
- Keep it entirely about how you evaluated your approach.

## Sanity Check
Ask: could you write this same section about someone else's proposed solution to the same problem, without knowing anything about their results? If yes, the separation of concerns is right.
