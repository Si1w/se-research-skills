# Threats to Validity

## Tone
- **When written badly**: reads like an apology.
- **When written well**: reads like a sincere attempt to encourage the reader to take up the research agenda and address limitations.

## Actionable Findings
- Think about the actionable conclusions from your research: what will change in future research or practice as a result?
- This helps you think about impact and helps the referee understand why they should care.
- If this is hard to write, it may be a sign the research needs more work.
- Even if you decide not to include it, writing it is a good intellectual discipline.
