# Project Name

## The Problem
Who is affected, what specifically breaks, how severe, what they do today without a solution.

## Why Now
What changed that makes this solvable or urgent — new data, new tools, new scale.

## Core Idea
The approach in one paragraph. Why this approach over alternatives.

## What Exists & Why It's Not Enough
Key existing work and why it doesn't solve the problem. Not a literature list — an argument.

## Experiment Design
Datasets, baselines, metrics, evaluation criteria. Be specific about what's available and what needs to be collected.

## Validation Plan
The minimum experiment to prove the core assumption. Data, metric, success/failure threshold.

## Risks & Open Questions
What could kill the project. Technical risks, data risks, feasibility concerns.

## TODO
Actionable next steps with rough ordering.
